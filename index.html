Campus Life



School Library

Was Build in 1962.

Library’s objective is to inculcate the habit of reading; children are introduced to the library and books right from the Kindergarten level. They realize the joys of reading as well as the importance of books, in their formative years itself.Reference books are also kept to help students with their course studies. The cataloging and issue procedure in the library is through library cards. Students at St. Xaviers are encouraged not onlyGroundsue reading but also to develop the art of story-telling, enacting and reading aloud.




School Ground

Was Build in 1914.

Open space, fresh air, voices cheering and spirits soaring is what you will see on our playgrounds. These days with little or no play area and time as well; we have ensured that our children do not miss out on it at school also. We have a huge play area which accommodates a football field.

School Achievements


Interschool Debate Competition
Won First Prize in Inter-school Debate Competition

This year the Interschool Debate Competition was held at St.Xaviers School. There were 24 different schools participating. The competition was held for a week. Food and accommodation were arranged at school canteen. All the participants were extraordinarily skilled.One could actually see the future leaders in the contestants. The decisions of the judges were final. There were no foul plays. This year St.Xaviers School won the first prize and much to our amazement two of the best speakers were from St.Xaviers School.All the participants were students with equal calibre and potential but in the end, some stood out shining brighter than the others. The topic for the final competition was " the increasing number of old age homes in India ". There were so many notable points presented by each team but the point presented by the winning team was the game-changer.











Inter-school Football Tournament
came Second in Inter-school Football Tournament

St.Xaviers School, Mumbai organised an Inter-school Football Tournament from 17th February, 2019 to 29th February, 2019 in the school grounds. Teams from many top schools across the city participated in the tournament.The tournament began with a match between the home team and last year's winning team from Amity International School. The Sports teachers Mr. Swaminathan and Miss Kripa Singhal supervised the tournament with the help of a talented team of sports captains and prefects.The prize distribution ceremony for the tournament was held on 29th February, 2019 in the school. The prizes were given away by the School Principal Ratna Shah. The team from Springdales School, Chanakyapuri stood first. The second and third position was grabbed by teams from St.Xaviers and Ramjas School respectively. The winners were applauded by the spectators. All participants received participation certificates and complimentary T-shirts from the sponsor Nike.
